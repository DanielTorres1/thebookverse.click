Our Philosophy: Diverse Stories & Wanderlust

At Books and Bao, we believe that stories have no borders. Reading translated literature opens a window into different minds, cultures, and landscapes, fostering empathy and connecting us all globally.

Connecting Through Translated Works

Translated literature makes up only a tiny fraction of published works in English-speaking nations, yet it holds the keys to understanding perspectives that are often overlooked. By reviewing and spotlighting indie translations—from Japanese magical realism to contemporary Latin American thriller authors—we aim to make international literature accessible and exciting for everyone.

The Art of Literary Wanderlust

Every place has its own story, written by local authors who live and breathe its history. Our travel guides don't just point you to standard tourist traps; they invite you to explore the literary heritage of a city. Whether it's visiting historic hotel libraries in Wales, browsing independent bookshops in Tokyo, or walking in the footsteps of your favorite writers in Edinburgh, we connect literature with place.
